Most guests will be joining us at The River Haven, a sprawling 11-bedroom home on the Guadalupe River.

Here are some highlights of the property:

  • 11 bedrooms, 5.5 bathrooms, 3 kitchens: Plenty of space for everyone.
  • Direct private river access: Perfect for kayaking, fishing, and swimming.
  • Huge shaded yard: Great for outdoor games and relaxing under the 120+ year old pecan shade trees.
  • Large fire pit: For evening s’mores and stories under the stars.




Fly in to San Antonio airport. River Haven is about 45 minutes outside of town. We'll coordinate pickups and/or Ubers closer to the event.
